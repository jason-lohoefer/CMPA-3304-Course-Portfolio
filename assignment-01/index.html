Top 5 Disney World Thrill Rides

My personal ranking of the best thrill rides at Walt Disney World.

  1. Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind

    This indoor roller coaster combines smooth movement, rotating ride vehicles, music, and impressive visual effects. It feels fast and unpredictable without being overly rough.

  2. TRON Lightcycle / Run

    Riders sit in a motorcycle-style position and launch onto a high-speed track. The futuristic design and nighttime lighting make it especially memorable.

  3. Rock 'n' Roller Coaster Starring The Muppets

    This indoor coaster begins with a powerful launch and includes several inversions. The dark setting, music, and Muppets theme make it a fun and intense ride.

  4. Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance

    This attraction combines multiple ride systems, detailed environments, and live-action storytelling. It feels more like entering a Star Wars adventure than riding a traditional theme park attraction.

  5. Space Mountain

    This classic indoor roller coaster uses darkness, sharp turns, and sudden drops to make the ride feel faster and more intense than it actually is.
